Bricabraque : uncopyright

en | fr

This tiny writing space on the Internet where I write sometimes is uncopyrighted in order to allow the freedom to read, share and modify its content for everyone. I release all claims on copyright that I am able to give up as the author by sharing you what I write under the terms of the Creative Commons Zero license (see the link below for more information). Indeed I think the aim of my “work”, assuming it is a little bit interesting, is to be shared. Therefore, I want to remove all the obstacles which may obstruct the sharing.

TL;DR: do what the fuck you want to.
